ORM

Online reputation management helps track, resolve, and minimize negative motions and comments in social media and results of search engine pages (SERPs). This begins with addressing negative reviews and responding to them in an honest and validating manner, and ends with tackling unfair comments in the fairest manner possible. A well-thought-out ORM helps you get back your reputation, and make you appear positive in the eyes of your customers.

  • - Identify negative reviews, comments, and blog posts
  • - Respond to these negative feedback with with genuine comments that are action-oriented
  • - When responding doesn’t work, consider alternative but ethical ways to address the issue
  • - Continuously track social media and online spaces for comments that might hurt your brand
